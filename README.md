## 📝 PROPOSED SOLUTION

Students in higher education face increasing mental health challenges due to academic pressure, stress, anxiety, and social responsibilities. Our proposed solution is a **digital mental wellness web platform** that provides accessible, engaging, and effective psychological support.

### Key Features:
- 🌐 Dedicated **web platform for student mental wellness**
- 🎭 **Comedy videos** in Telugu, Hindi, and English for stress relief
- 🧘 **Meditation and breathing exercises** for relaxation
- 🔊 **Embedded audio content** for seamless listening
- 🔁 **Easy navigation** with Home and Back buttons
- 📈 **Scalable design** for adding future wellness content

### Expected Outcome:
- Reduced stress and anxiety
- Improved mood and emotional balance
- Enhanced mental well-being of students
